# Dashboard Design

## Dashboard Objective

Provide operational visibility into application demand, customer concentration, transaction mix, and regional workload distribution.

Target Users:

- Operations Managers
- Business Analysts
- Regional Managers
- Executive Stakeholders

---

# Page 1: Executive Overview

## KPIs

- Total Applications
- Monthly Growth Rate
- Top Customer Share
- Regional Concentration Ratio
- Search-to-Application Ratio

## Visuals

- KPI Cards
- Monthly Trend Line
- Regional Demand Map
- Transaction Mix Donut Chart

## Key Questions

- Is application demand increasing?
- Which regions generate the most workload?
- Which transaction types dominate activity?

---

# Page 2: Customer Analysis

## KPIs

- Active Customers
- Top 10 Customer Share
- Top 1% Customer Share

## Visuals

- Customer Ranking Bar Chart
- Pareto Chart
- Customer Segmentation Chart

## Key Questions

- How concentrated is customer activity?
- Which customers generate the largest workload?
- Is there customer concentration risk?

---

# Page 3: Regional Analysis

## KPIs

- Regional Volume
- Growth by Region

## Visuals

- Region Ranking
- Heat Map
- Monthly Regional Trends

## Key Questions

- Which regions generate the most applications?
- Which regions are growing fastest?
- Where should resources be allocated?

---

# Page 4: Transaction Analysis

## KPIs

- OC1 Volume
- DLG Volume
- FR Volume
- Transaction Mix Share

## Visuals

- Transaction Distribution
- Monthly Transaction Trends
- Stacked Area Chart

## Key Questions

- Which transaction types dominate activity?
- Has transaction mix changed over time?

---

# Recommended Filters

- Date
